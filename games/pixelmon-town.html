Pixelmon Town

Welcome to Pixelmon Town, a charming pixel art adventure where you can collect, train, and battle with unique monsters! Explore a vibrant world filled with secrets, meet other trainers, and build your ultimate monster collection.

How to Play Pixelmon Town?

Use arrow keys or WASD to move around the town. Click or press spacebar to interact with objects and characters. Catch monsters by engaging them in battle, train them to become stronger, and challenge other trainers. Collect items, complete quests, and discover new areas as you progress through the game.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I catch new monsters?

You can catch monsters by encountering them in the wild and winning battles. Use your starter monster to weaken wild ones, then throw a capture device to add them to your collection.

Can I evolve my monsters?

Yes! Monsters can evolve as they gain experience and level up. Some monsters have multiple evolution stages with different requirements.

Are there different areas to explore?

The game features various areas including forests, caves, and special locations. Each area has unique monsters to discover and challenges to overcome.

How do I make my monsters stronger?

Train your monsters by battling wild monsters and other trainers. You can also use special items to enhance their abilities and teach them new moves.
