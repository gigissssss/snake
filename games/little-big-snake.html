Little Big Snake

Little Big Snake is an exciting multiplayer snake game where you control a colorful snake in a vibrant world. Grow longer by collecting food, avoid obstacles and other snakes, and compete to become the longest snake in the arena. With beautiful graphics and smooth controls, this game offers an engaging experience for players of all ages.

How to Play Little Big Snake?

1. Use arrow keys or WASD to control your snake's movement
2. Collect glowing orbs to grow longer and earn points
3. Avoid colliding with walls, obstacles, and other snakes
4. Try to trap other snakes by surrounding them
5. Use speed boosts strategically to escape or catch other snakes
6. Compete with players worldwide to climb the leaderboard
